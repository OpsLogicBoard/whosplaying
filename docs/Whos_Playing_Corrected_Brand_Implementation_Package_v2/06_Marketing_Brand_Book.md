# 06 - Marketing Brand Book v2.0

## Brand Story
Who's Playing began as a local effort to make live music easier to find in Jacksonville Beach and neighboring communities.

It is growing into a platform that helps:
- Fans find shows
- Artists build audiences
- Venues fill rooms
- Communities support local music

## Voice
- Friendly
- Clear
- Local
- Helpful
- Music-first

## Messaging Pillars
1. Find live music near you.
2. Support local artists.
3. Fill local venues.
4. Make every community easier to explore.

## Tagline
LIVE MUSIC. LOCAL PEOPLE. REAL CONNECTION.

## Social Tone
Use short, inviting, direct copy.

Examples:
- Tonight’s live music, without the digging.
- Find the room before the first song starts.
- Support the artists. Fill the rooms.
